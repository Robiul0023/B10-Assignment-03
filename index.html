Green home construction, or eco-friendly house building, is all about creating houses that are better for the environment. It's a smart and forward-thinking way of building homes, especially important in a world where taking care of our planet is a top priority.

Concept

The most notable advantage of green building is its positive impact on the environment. By reducing water usage and minimising the reliance on non-renewable energy sources like fossil fuels, green buildings help protect our atmosphere and natural surroundings.
